---
title: "Use templates"
description: "Start from an existing workflow instead of creating every agent from scratch."
---

Templates are a fast way to discover what Dataleap can do and adapt a working workflow to your own process.

## Start from a template

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Templates">

In the sidebar, click **Templates**.

</Step>
<Step title="Browse the library">

Open a template that looks close to the job you want to automate.

</Step>
<Step title="Review the workflow">

Each template helps you inspect what the workflow does, the detailed steps, and the connected apps it expects.

</Step>
<Step title="Build from the template">

Click the template's build action to open a pre-filled **New Agent** flow.

</Step>
<Step title="Customize before you finish">

Adjust the prompt, add your own context, and then build the new agent.

</Step>
</Steps>

## What templates are good for

- Learning how a workflow is structured.
- Reusing a working starting point.
- Exploring supported apps and common patterns.

## What to customize

Before you rely on a template, update:

- The business context
- The apps and accounts it should use
- The output format
- Any steps that do not match your process

<Tip>
  Treat templates as a starting point, not a finished setup.
</Tip>
