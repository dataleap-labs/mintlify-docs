---
title: "Connect apps"
description: "Connect external services so your agents can read from and act in the tools you already use."
---

Connected apps let your agents work with the systems where your data already lives.

## Where to connect apps

You can connect apps from two places:

- An individual agent, through the **Connected Apps** section in the side panel.
- Global settings, through **Settings** and then **Connected Apps**.

## Connect an app for an agent

<Steps>
<Step title="Open the agent">

Go to **Agents** and open the agent you want to update.

</Step>
<Step title="Open Connected Apps">

In the side panel, look for **Connected Apps**.

</Step>
<Step title="Browse or search the supported apps">

Select the services the agent should use.

</Step>
<Step title="Finish the connection flow">

Follow the prompts for the app you selected, then return to the agent.

</Step>
</Steps>

## Manage existing connections

In **Settings** > **Connected Apps**, you can:

- Search your current connections.
- Review which agents use a connection.
- Reconnect or remove a connection when needed.

If your workspace uses teams, you may see separate personal and team connections.

## Best practices

- Connect only the apps the agent actually needs.
- Re-test the agent after you add a new connection.
- Remove stale connections so you do not lose track of what an agent can access.

<Tip>
  If you are building from scratch, connect the apps early so the agent can use real tools while you refine its instructions.
</Tip>
