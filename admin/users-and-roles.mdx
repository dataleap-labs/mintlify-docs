Admins get their own area at `app.dataleap.ai/admin`. The **Management** section covers who's in the workspace and what applies to everyone.

## Members & roles

Invite people, assign roles, and manage access:

- **Roles:** every member is an **Admin** or a **Member** — switchable per person.
- **Team admins:** members can additionally administer specific teams (shown per user).
- **Invite Members** handles new joiners; pending requests and invites have their own tabs.

## Organization defaults

The **Organization** page sets what applies to every agent in the workspace:

- **Workspace defaults** — display currency and the timezone used for analytics and team runs.
- **System prompt** — organization-level instructions **applied to every agent in the organization** (up to 12,000 characters). The place for company-wide rules: working language, PII handling, tone. Builders never have to remember them; every agent gets them automatically.
