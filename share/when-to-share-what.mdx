---
title: "When to share what"
description: "A simple decision guide: personal agent, team agent, template, locked template — or a skill."
---

Value compounds when agents stop being personal and start being shared. Use this table to pick the right way:

| Choose | When | Details |
|---|---|---|
| 🧍 **Personal agent** | You're experimenting, the workflow only serves you, or it works with personal data (your inbox, your notes). | Stays private in your [personal space](/share/team-spaces). |
| 👥 **Team agent** | The whole team profits — everyone should see, run, and edit it. | Lives in a [team space](/share/team-spaces), on team credentials and team budget. |
| 📋 **Template** | Others should clone a proven workflow and adapt it to their context. | [Open template](/share/templates) — fully editable clones. |
| 🔒 **Locked template** | Many people must run the **identical** process. | [Locked template](/share/locked-templates) — owner updates reach every clone. |
| 🧩 **Skill** | A standard or definition that 2+ agents should follow. | [Skill](/scale/skills) — write it once; personal, team, or org-wide. |
